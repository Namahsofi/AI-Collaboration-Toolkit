# ACT Vision

## Our Vision

Create a future where effective collaboration with AI is a learnable, transferable methodology available to every mission-driven organization.

We envision a world where organizations no longer depend on one "AI expert" or one software platform. Instead, they build lasting organizational capability through repeatable collaboration practices.

The AI Collaboration Toolkit (ACT) empowers nonprofits, educators, founders, community organizations, and other mission-driven teams to collaborate with AI confidently, responsibly, and effectively.

## Long-Term Goals

- Make AI collaboration a universal professional skill.
- Reduce AI Capability Debt across organizations.
- Build an open ecosystem of reusable AI Collaboration Workflows (ACWs).
- Keep human judgment at the center of every AI-assisted decision.
- Create a methodology that outlives any single AI model or software platform.

## Success Looks Like

Organizations don't just produce better AI-assisted work—they become better collaborators with AI over time.

Knowledge is shared, workflows are reusable, and AI capability becomes an organizational asset rather than an individual skill.
