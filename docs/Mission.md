# ACT Mission

## Mission Statement

ACT exists because many organizations doing some of the world's most important work have the fewest resources to effectively collaborate with AI.

Our mission is to provide an open, repeatable methodology that helps people and organizations collaborate responsibly and effectively with AI while continuously improving their own capabilities.

## Our Commitments

- Teach AI collaboration, not just prompting.
- Keep humans responsible for final decisions.
- Build reusable collaboration workflows.
- Create open-source resources anyone can improve.
- Reduce AI Capability Debt.
- Make AI collaboration accessible to everyone.

## How We Measure Success

Success is measured by:

- Increased human capability
- Better collaboration practices
- Reusable organizational knowledge
- Higher-quality outcomes
- Sustainable AI adoption
