# AI Collaboration Toolkit (ACT)

> An open-source methodology and platform for helping mission-driven organizations collaborate effectively with AI through structured AI Collaboration Workflows (ACWs).

---

## Vision

ACT exists because many organizations doing the world's most important work have the fewest resources to adopt AI effectively.

Rather than teaching prompt engineering alone, ACT teaches a repeatable methodology for human-AI collaboration that improves both outcomes and human capability over time.

---

## The ACT Framework

Every AI Collaboration Workflow (ACW) follows the same six-stage methodology:

1. 🔍 Discover
2. 📝 Prepare
3. 🤝 Collaborate
4. ✅ Review
5. ✨ Refine
6. ♻️ Reuse

The framework ensures AI is used as a collaborative thinking partner while keeping human judgment at the center.

---

## Current MVP

The first release focuses on mission-driven organizations.

Initial AI Collaboration Workflows include:

- Grant Proposal Development
- Board Meeting Preparation
- Impact Reporting
- Donor Communications

---

## Project Goals

- Teach effective AI collaboration through real work
- Reduce AI Capability Debt in organizations
- Build reusable AI Collaboration Workflows
- Keep humans in control of important decisions
- Create an open-source methodology anyone can improve

---

## Why ACT?

Most AI failures are not technology failures.

They are collaboration failures.

ACT provides structure that helps people:

- ask better questions
- gather better context
- review AI critically
- improve outputs
- reuse successful workflows
